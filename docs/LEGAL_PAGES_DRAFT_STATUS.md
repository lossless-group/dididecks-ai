# Legal Pages Draft Status

The routes:

- `/legal/privacy`
- `/legal/terms`

are draft product pages only.

They exist to make the DidiDecks product presentable and to establish the expected content model for production legal pages.

They are not final legal text.

Before production deployment, these pages require:

- legal review
- jurisdiction review
- billing language review
- AI processing language review
- retention and privacy review

The frontend should never imply that these drafts are final approved legal terms until that review is complete.
